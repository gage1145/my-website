    Professional Summary

    Welcome to my website! I am a researcher and data scientist working in the fields of prion biology, software development, and laboratory automation. With over a decade of experience, I have developed skills around designing analytical pipelines, building reproducible tools, and turning experimental data into publishable results.

    My work spans protein engineering, laboratory automation, R package development, statistical modeling, and full-stack development of laboratory information management systems (LIMS). I care deeply about scientific clarity, reproducibility, and building things that get used; whether that’s science, software, or products.

    Fun fact, I make music under the name GANYMEDE. You can find a collection of curated songs and audio projects on the Music page.
